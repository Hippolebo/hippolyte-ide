## ğŸ’¡ Methodology

### Screening Criteria
1. Listed on primary stock exchange
2. Positive earnings (P/E ratio calculable)
3. Ranked by P/E ratio (lowest to highest)
4. Select top 10 (bottom 10 by P/E)

### Portfolio Construction
- **Equal-weighted:** 10% allocation per stock
- **Total per country:** â‚¬10,000 hypothetical portfolio
- **Rebalancing:** Quarterly review
- **Holding period:** Minimum 1 quarter, maximum 1 year

### Analysis Framework
For each country, I analyze:
- Company fundamentals (margins, ROE, debt levels)
- Sector composition
- Dividend yields and sustainability
- Value trap risks
- Forward earnings expectations
